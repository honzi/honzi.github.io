Honzi/Bookmarks/EVE/Corpses
  • Corpse price: 1m each
  • Corpses are traditionally sold in packs of 10 for 10m, with 4 females and 6 males per pack, without any duplicates. The 4:6 ratio is based on supply and demand.
  • Corpses are selected pseudorandomly. Corpses with duplicates are selected 1st until not enough duplicates remain, then any corpse can be selected.
  • Bulk corpses can also be purchased or sold in Amarr local for the same price of 1m each, with duplicates being allowed and unavoidable. Must be all or nothing without excluding any corpse.
