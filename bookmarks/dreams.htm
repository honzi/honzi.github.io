Honzi/Bookmarks/Multiverse Dreams
Rank Status Notes
1 Strong
  • Good restful sleep.
  • Vivid dreams that can be relatively easily remembered and explained.
2 Weak
  • Mostly good restful sleep.
  • Difficulty remembering and explaining dreams, or no dreams at all.
3 Garbled
  • Difficult and no more than decent sleep, often with feelings of anxiety and frustration.
  • Vivid dreams that make no sense even within typical dream physics, often impossible to explain even to self.
4 Blocked
  • Very unrestful sleep, frequent insomnia.
  • Little chance for dreams, but weak/garbled dreams can occur if sufficient time to overcome insomnia.
