honzi.cz/Writings/Chat Etiquette
  • Do not assume that anybody can read your mind:
    • Say `afk` if you are leaving for a medium+ amount of time, with an optional number of hours and/or minutes.
    • Say `brb` if you are leaving for a small amount of time, with an optional number of minutes and/or seconds.
  • Treat chat as you would an improv:
    • A less restrictive version of `yes, and` keeps the conversation healthy.
    • Make every participant `look good`.
