Honzi/Guides/Dungeon Siege 2
  • Combat Magic Builds:
    • Death
    • Damage over Time
    • Fire
    • Lightning
  • Melee Builds:
    • 2-Handed
    • Dual Wield
    • Shield
  • Nature Magic Builds:
    • Healing
    • Ice
    • Summoner
  • Ranged Builds:
    • Bows
    • Thrown
  • Farming
  • Always recite a chant when you reach an Incantation Shrine. Prosperity chants are a generally good option.
  • Be careful when changing weapons or spells in your spell book, for your character will attack with the most recently changed weapon/spell unless you manually change back to which attack you prefer.
  • If you cannot pick up an item automatically, try picking it up with a click when your inventory is open.
  • If you need health or mana but don't have any potions, park your characters in a safe area and take a break to stretch your legs. Make sure your game isn't paused.
  • Spells are contained within spell books. If you are upgrading your spell book, make sure to move all the spells out 1st.
  • Spells that instantly hit an enemy without travel time can be cast thru walls.
  • Statue Guardian: Azunai, Xeria, Elandir, Zaramoth. Get it wrong 5 times for additional boss monsters.
