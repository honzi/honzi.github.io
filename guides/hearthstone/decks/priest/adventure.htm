Honzi/Guides/Hearthstone/Adventure Priest
This deck is designed to easily beat many normal and heroic bosses in "Solo Adventures" mode by using a 5-mana 5-card combo that only requires 280 dust.
Playing:
  • Hard mulligan for pieces of the 5-card combo.
  • Do not play pieces of the combo until you have all of them.
  • Survive until at least turn 5 and execute the combo:
    1. Play Knife Juggler.
    2. Play Radiant Elemental.
    3. Play Test Subject.
    4. Cast Embalmbing Ritual on the Test Subject.
    5. Cast Holy Smite on the Test Subject.
    6. Repeat steps 4, 5, and 6 until the enemy boss is defeated.
  • Try to survive as long as possible to get the combo, but restart the game once there is no hope. Eventually you will succeed.
Critical cards:
Embalmbing Ritual 1 Common Priest SoU
Holy Smite 1 Priest Basic
Knife Juggler 2 Rare Neutral Classic
Radiant Elemental 2 Common Priest JtU
Test Subject 1 Rare Priest TBP
Some alternate cards can be used to achieve the combo, but require additional dust. Include them if you have them:
Forbidden Words 0 Rare Priest RoS Alternate for Holy Smite.
Topsy Turvy 0 Common Priest TBP Alternate for Holy Smite.
