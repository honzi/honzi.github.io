Honzi/Guides/Hearthstone/Renounce Darkness Warlock
Playing:
  • Hard mulligan for Renounce Darkness.
  • The coin should not be used for additional card draw, since it becomes a lot better after Renounce Darkness is used. You can use the coin to reduce your hand size from 10 to 9 at the end of your turn, since the mana cost of the Warlock card you draw will be reduced by 1 anyway.
  • After you have played Renounce Darkness, avoid picking another one when discovering cards. It only replaces Warlock cards, which you no longer have.
