Honzi/Guides/Hearthstone/Shifter Zerus Tavern Brawl
  • If your opponent does not have a threatening board and your Shifter Zeruses aren't very good, you should wait a turn for better shift results.
  • Mulligan:
    • It is typically best to mulligan for a hand full of only Shifter Zerus.
    • Some random class spells can be worth keeping, such as card draw or powerful board clears.
  • Since all spells are random and potentially not very impactful, board control is the primary way to victory.
  • Use as few Shifter Zeruses as possible to maintain board control, for having significantly less options than your opponent will put you at a big disadvantage.
