Honzi/Guides/Hearthstone/"Fun" Random
This list includes cards that subjectively qualify as suitable for playing without cards that remove "fun" from your opponent.
  • Playing the exact same deck as your opponent makes games the most fair.
  • Prince Malchazaar must be included in your deck.
  • When discovering cards, pick cards that are on this list. If none are available, choose the most entertaining/interesting cards.
Only cards up to the Forged in the Barrens expansion are currently included.
