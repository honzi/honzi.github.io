Honzi/Guides/World of Warcraft/Restrictions
  • You cannot carry more than 1,000 gold, which limits what you can purchase.
  • You cannot chat in certain channels, such as zone General and LocalDefense channels.
  • You cannot join or create a guild.
  • You cannot level your character past level 20.
  • You cannot send mail or access any mail that you have received.
  • You cannot send private messages to other players.
  • You cannot trade with another player, even if they initiate the trade.
  • You cannot use any auction house.
