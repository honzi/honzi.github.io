Honzi/Guides/World of Warcraft/Bags
Bag Progression:
  1. Death Knights start the game with 4 12 slot bags.
  2. 14 slot [Thick Hide Pack]
  3. 18 slot [Explorer's Pack]:
    • Alliance: vendor on the ship that travels between Stormwind and Kul Tiras.
    • Horde: vendor in The Sliver in Zuldazar.
  4. Demon Hunters start the game with 4 20 slot bags and can find a 28 slot bag in Mardum.
Bag Contents:
  • Currencies you are collecting or need to carry:
    • [Darkmoon Game Token]: at least 100
    • [Mark of Honor]
  • [Darkmoon Adventerer's Guide]
  • Fishing Equipment:
    • Fishing Hat:
      1. [Weather-Beaten Fishing Hat]: reward from fishing daily quests.
    • Fishing Pole:
      1. [Fishing Pole]: sold by fishing supplies vendors.
      2. [Strong Fishing Pole]: sold by fishing supplies vendors.
      3. [Bone Fishing Pole]: reward from fishing daily quests.
  • Full set of gear for each of your class's specializations.
  • [Hearthstone]
  • Your faction has interesting quest items that you should carry:
    • Alliance: 2 quest items
    • Horde: 2 quest items
