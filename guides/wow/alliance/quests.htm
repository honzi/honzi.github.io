Honzi/Guides/World of Warcraft/Alliance Quests
Quest Continent Zone Notes
Extinguish the Fires Eastern Kingdoms Dun Morogh Gives your character [Firefighting Gear] which remains visible everywhere.
Nature's Reprisal Kalimdor Teldrassil Gives [Ireroot Seeds] which can be thrown anywhere.
The Great Moongraze Hunt Kalimdor Azuremyst Isle Gives you the Roasted Moongraze Tenderloin cooking recipe.
Writings of the Void Kalimdor Darkshore Jump into the Maw of the Void and kill the quest mob. Gives you the Ghosts in the Dark achievement.
