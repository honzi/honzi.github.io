Draenei Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Gemcutting: +10 Jewelcrafting.
  • Gift of the Naaru: heal target for 20% of your maximum health over 5 seconds, 3 minute cooldown.
  • Heroic Presense: +3 agility/intellect/strength at level 20.
  • Shadow Resistance: shadow damage taken reduced by 1%.
Dwarf Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Explorer: find additional fragments during Archaeology, faster survey speed.
  • Frost Resistance: frost damage taken reduced by 1%.
  • Might of the Mountain: +2% critical damage and healing.
  • Stoneform: remove bleed/curse/disease/magic/poison effects from yourself, physical damage taken reduced by 10% for 8 seconds, 2 minute cooldown.
Gnome Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Arcane Resistance: arcane damage taken reduced by 1%.
  • Engineering Specialization: +15 Engineering.
  • Expansive Mind: +5% maxiumum energy/focus/mana/rage, depending on your class.
  • Escape Artist: free yourself from immobilization and movement speed reduction effects, 1 minute cooldown.
  • Nimble Fingers: +1% haste.
Human Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Diplomacy: +10% reputation gains.
  • Every Man for Himself: remove stun effects, 3 minute cooldown.
  • The Human Spirit: +2% crit/haste/versality from all sources.
Night Elf Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Elusiveness: +5% movement speed while stealthed.
  • Nature Resistance: nature damage taken reduced by 1%.
  • Quickness: +2% chance to dodge melee and ranged attacks, +2% movement speed.
  • Shadowmeld: stealth until cancelled or movement, 2 minute cooldown.
  • Touch of Elune: +1% crit during the day, +1% haste during the night.
  • Wisp Spirit: +75% movement speed while dead.
Pandaren Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Bouncy: 50% reduced falling damage taken.
  • Epicurean: +100% increased Well Fed bonuses.
  • Gourmand: +15 Cooking.
  • Inner Peace: +100% increased rested experience.
  • Quaking Palm: incapacitate enemy for 4 seconds, melee range, 2 minute cooldown.
Worgen Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Abberation: nature and shadow damage taken reduced by 1%.
  • Darkflight: +40% movement speed for 10 seconds, 2 minute cooldown.
  • Flayer: +15 Skinning, faster skinning speed.
  • Running Wild: free "mount".
  • Two Forms: can switch between human form and worgen form.
  • Viciousness: +1% crit.
