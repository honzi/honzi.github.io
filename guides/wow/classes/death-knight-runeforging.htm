Runeforge Notes
Rune of Razorice
  • +2% weapon damage as frost
  • +3% damage dealt by your frost attacks to target for 25 seconds, stacking up to 5 times
Rune of the Fallen Crusader your attacks have chance to heal 6% health and gain +15% strength for 15 seconds
Rune of the Stoneskin Gargoyle
  • +5% armor
  • +5% stamina
  • +5% strength
Rune of Unending Thirst
  • +5% movement speed
  • when you kill an enemy, heal 5% health and gain +10% haste and movement speed for 10 seconds
