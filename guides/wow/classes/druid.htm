Honzi/Guides/World of Warcraft/Druid
  • Recommended Alliance race: Night Elf
  • Recommended Horde race: Tauren
  • Armor: Prefers leather, can wear cloth.
  • Speak to Lorelae Wintersong in the Nighthaven Inn in Moonglade to purchase class tomes for 50g each:
    • Tome of the Wilds: Charm Woodland Creature
    • Tome of the Wilds: Mount Form
    • Tome of the Wilds: Track Beasts
    • Tome of the Wilds: Treant Form
  • Druids can use the Nature's Beacon toy to teleport randomly to:
    • Bough Shadow, Ashenvale, Kalimdor
    • Golakka Hot Springs, Un'Goro Crater, Kalimdor
    • Grizzlemaw, Grizzly Hills, Northrend
    • Grove of the Ancients, Darkshore, Kalimdor
    • Jade Forest (?), Pandaria
    • Jademir Lake, Feralas, Kalimdor
    • Lunarwing Shallows, Val'sharah, Broken Isles
    • Moonglade (south-southeast), Kalimdor
    • Oracle Glade, Teldrassil, Kalimdor
    • Orendil's Retreat, Ashenvale, Kalimdor
    • Paoquan Hollow, Valley of the Four Winds, Pandaria
    • Seradane, Hinterlands, Eastern Kingdoms
    • Sholazar Basin (north-northwest), Northrend
    • Tainted Forest, Blasted Lands, Eastern Kingdoms
    • Twilight Grove, Duskwood, Eastern Kingdoms
    • Whispering Forest, Tirisfal Glades, Eastern Kingdoms
  • Recommended exploration spec: Restoration
