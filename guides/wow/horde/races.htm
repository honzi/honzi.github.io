Blood Elf Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Arcane Acuity: +1% crit.
  • Arcane Affinity: +10 Enchanting.
  • Arcane Resistance: arcane damage taken reduced by 1%.
  • Arcane Torrent: restore 15 energy/15 focus/3% mana/15 rage, depending on class, remove 1 benefitial effect from all enemies within 8 yards, 2 minute cooldown.
Goblin Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Best Deals Anywhere: get best possible gold discount regardless of faction reputation standing.
  • Better Living Through Chemistry: +15 Alchemy.
  • Rocket Barrage: launch rockets at enemy, 30 yard range, 1.5 minute cooldown. Cooldown shared with Rocket Jump.
  • Rocket Jump: jump forward, 1.5 minute cooldown. Cooldown shared with Rocket Barrage.
  • Time is Money: +1% haste.
Orc Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Blood Fury: +13 attack power for 15 seconds, 2 minute cooldown.
  • Command: pet damage increased by 1%.
  • Hardiness: stun duration reduced by 20%.
Pandaren Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Bouncy: 50% reduced falling damage taken.
  • Epicurean: +100% increased Well Fed bonuses.
  • Gourmand: +15 Cooking.
  • Inner Peace: +100% increased rested experience.
  • Quaking Palm: incapacitate enemy for 4 seconds, melee range, 2 minute cooldown.
Tauren Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Brawn: +2% critical damage and healing.
  • Cultivation: +15 Herbalism, faster herb gathering speed.
  • Endurance: +9 stamina at level 20.
  • Nature Resistance: nature damage taken reduced by 1%.
  • War Stomp: stun up to 5 enemies within 8 yards for 2 seconds, 1.5 minute cooldown.
Troll Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Beast Slaying: +20% experience gained from killing beasts.
  • Berserking: +10% haste for 12 seconds, 3 minute cooldown.
  • Da Voodoo Shuffle: movement impairing duration reduced by 20%.
  • Regeneration: +10% health regeneration, 10% health regeneration continues during combat.
Undead Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes Yes
  • Cannibalize: restore 7% health/mana every 2 seconds for 10 seconds, requires humanoid or undead corpse, 2 minute cooldown.
  • Shadow Resistance: shadow damage taken reduced by 1%.
  • Touch of the Grave: attacks/spells have a chance to drain life from the target, can breathe underwater indefinitely.
  • Will of the Forsaken: remove charm/fear/sleep effects, 2 minute cooldown.
