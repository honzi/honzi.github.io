Honzi/Guides/World of Warcraft/Horde Quests
Quest Continent Zone Notes
A Growing Problem Kalimdor Northern Barrens Gives [Tonga's Eyes]:
  • Unlimited uses in any zone, 1 hour duration, 1 minute cooldown.
  • Summons Tonga's Eyes, an eagle guardian that follows you around.
Might Makes Right Kalimdor Ashenvale and Stonetalon Mountains Unlocks the Krom'gar Quartermaster vendor, which sells good items.
Never Trust a Big Barb and a Smile Kalimdor Durotar Gives [Poison Extraction Totem]:
  • Unlimited uses in any zone, 15 second duration, 15 second cooldown.
  • Can distract enemy tab targeting.
  • Has health equal to your max health when you cast it. Can be healed.
