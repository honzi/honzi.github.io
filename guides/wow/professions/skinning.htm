Honzi/Guides/World of Warcraft/Professions/Skinning
  • Carry a [Skinning Knife] to improve your skinning. You can purchase one from a leatherworking supplies vendor.
  • Skinning is a good second profession to get to help you train and gather supplies for leatherworking.
  • Worgen get +15 skinning and faster skinning speed as a racial bonus.
