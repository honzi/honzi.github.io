Installation:
  1. Go to "about:debugging".
  2. In the "This Firefox" section, click on "Load Temporary Add-on...".
  3. Select the "manifest.json" file of the extension that will be installed.
Uninstallation:
  1. Go to "about:debugging".
  2. In the "This Firefox" section, click on the "Remove" button of the extension you wish to uninstall.
  3. Extensions are also uninstalled automatically when you close the browser.
