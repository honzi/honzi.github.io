Honzi/Guides/Terraria
  • Base Building:
    • Build a base at the initial spawn location, for you will return there when you load the world or die.
    • Put an object that you can move thru, such as a platform, on the inside of your main entrance doors. This will prevent enemies from opening them from the outside.
    • Utilize the following in every base:
      • Campfire: increased life regeneration.
      • Cooking Pot: crafting.
      • Glass Kiln: crafting.
      • Heavy Work Bench: crafting.
      • Work Bench: crafting.
    • Utilize optional items if their effects are desired:
      • Mushroom Grass: Glowing Mushrooms and Mushroom Seeds.
      • Sunflowers: increased movement speed and reduced enemy spawn rate.
  • Enable "UseExperimentalFeatures" to see and set world generation seeds. This allows you to scout ahead or farm materials in disposable copies of your main world.
  • Entering a created world spawns you at the same spawn location as when it was 1st created, regardless of your location when you saved and exited.
  • Renewable Items
  • Take your coins out of a chest and then put them back into the chest to properly stack coins without making additional stacks.
  • Your character's inventory remains constant, even when switching to different worlds. This results in unlimited of every resource, as long as you are willing to gather them in a different world.
