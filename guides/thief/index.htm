Thief 3: Deadly Shadows
  • Cancel your drawn bow without shooting an arrow by using the jump key.
  • Create a save file before the end of a level to quickly start playing the level after it whenever you want.
  • Edit Default.ini to modify your game experience.
  • If your animations get stuck, take out your bow, draw any arrow, then cancel it.
  • Toggle fullscreen: Alt + Enter
