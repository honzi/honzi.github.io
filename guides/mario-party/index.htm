Honzi/Guides/Mario Party
When farming coins, you should maximize the net worth of all 4 players:
  • Do not pay coins for any actions that don't result in net gain of coins, such as paying coins to steal coins from another player.
  • Enable bonus stars.
  • Go for extra coins in minigames whenever possible.
  • Only purchase items if they are needed to get stars, such as skeleton keys.
  • Stars should be purchased as much as possible.
  • The group of 3 players in 1v3 minigames should win.
