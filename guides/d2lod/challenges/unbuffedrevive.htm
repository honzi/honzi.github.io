Honzi/Guides/Diablo 2: Lord of Destruction/UnbuffedRevive Necromancer Challenge
  • Download: UnbuffedRevive.d2s (~2KB)
  • Play a character that can only revive fallen enemies.
  • Rules:
    • If you die, you must save and exit game.
    • You cannot hire a mercenary.
    • You cannot unequip the items that are equipped.
    • You cannot use any potions.
    • You cannot use any skill or stat points.
    • You can only swing your wand and use Revive.
