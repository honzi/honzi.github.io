Honzi/Guides/Diablo 2: Lord of Destruction/OnHit Assassin Challenge
  • Download: onhit.d2s (~2KB)
  • Play a character that always hits but must rely on numerous 1% chances of interesting on-hit effects.
  • Rules:
    • If you die, you must save and exit game.
    • You cannot hire a mercenary.
    • You cannot unequip the items that are equipped.
    • You cannot use any potions.
    • You cannot use any skill or stat points.
