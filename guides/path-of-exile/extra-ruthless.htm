Honzi/Guides/Path of Exile/Extra Ruthless
  • You must play as hardcore solo self-found.
  • Drop all starting flasks and scrolls.
  • Cannot share stash with any other characters, including in the same league.
  • Never buy any equippable items from vendors.
  • Must complete available quests and trials of ascendancy before moving on to the next area.
  • Reset all instances after each level up, quest, and waypoint.
