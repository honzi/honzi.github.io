Fittings Code Notes
Security Missions 3,4 Alpha
  • Replace the shield resistance modules based upon the damage you will be receiving and leave the others in your mission station.
  • If you need additional speed, use the afterburner and 1 multispectrum shield hardener.
  • Upgrade your drone damage amplifiers and shield extenders to abyssal for additional stats and reduced zkill value. Mutated drones get attacked more often and therefore should be avoided.
  • Use the nosferatu to warp while neuted.
  • Use the target painter to aggro enemies.
Security Missions 3,4 Omega
