Fittings Code Notes
Security Missions 3,4 Alpha
  • The Dominix Navy Issue has stronger versions of these fits.
  • Replace the medium and large drones based upon the damage you will be doing:
    • EM: Imperial Navy Infiltrator and Imperial Navy Praetor.
    • Explosive: Republic Fleet Valkyrie and Republic Fleet Berserker.
    • Kinetic: Caldari Navy Vespa and Caldari Navy Wasp.
    • Thermal: Federation Navy Hammerhead and Federation Navy Ogre.
  • Replace the shield resistance modules based upon the damage you will be receiving and leave the others in your mission station.
  • If you need additional speed, use the afterburner and 1 multispectrum shield hardener.
  • Upgrade your drone damage amplifiers and shield extenders to abyssal for additional stats and reduced zkill value. Mutated drones get attacked more often and therefore should be avoided.
  • Use the Hobgoblin Is as disposable drones to destroy things that deal damage when exploding.
  • Use the nosferatu to warp while neuted.
