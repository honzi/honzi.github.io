Honzi/Guides/EVE Online/Project Discovery
General Project Discovery Notes:
  • Each character on your account can independently complete Project Discovery.
  • Keep your accuracy rating as high as possible, for you will get bigger ISK rewards.
  • There is no penalty for closing the window after you have already seen the sample.
  • You can continue Project Discovery even after obtaining all the rewards.
Flow Cytometry:
  • Drawing polygons with 4 sides is typically good enough.
