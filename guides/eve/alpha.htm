Honzi/Guides/EVE Online/Alpha Clones
  • Never use Cerebral Accelerators to boost your attributes, as you will reach the maximum 5 million skillpoints within a few months of constant training.
  • After you reach 5 million skillpoints, alternate methods are required:
    • CCP Daily Rewards and Events: free, but slow.
    • Daily Alpha Injectors: cost 20 PLEX in the store and can be purchased on the market. Only once per day.
  • Alpha clones cannot set their safety to red in highsec.
  • Skills not listed in the table below cannot be trained in any way.
