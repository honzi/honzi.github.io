Honzi/Guides/EVE Online/Alpha Clones
  • Never use Cerebral Accelerators to boost your attributes. Boosted attributes do not help you after you max your skillpoints at 5 million within a few months of constant training, so it is always better to keep them or sell them.
  • Alpha clones only train 5 million skill points before alternate methods are required:
    • CCP Events/Giveaways: free, but slow.
    • Daily Alpha Injectors: cost 20 PLEX in the store.
  • Alpha clones cannot set their safety to red in highsec.
