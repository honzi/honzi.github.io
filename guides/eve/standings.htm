Honzi/Guides/EVE Online/Standings
  • Broker fees can be reduced by standings >0:
    • Corp standing multiplied by 0.02, up to 0.2% at 10.
    • Faction standing multiplied by 0.03, up to 0.3% at 10.
  • Level 1 Agents are always available.
  • Reprocessing taxes can be reduced by standings >0:
    • Corp standing divided by 1.334, up to 5% at 6.67.
