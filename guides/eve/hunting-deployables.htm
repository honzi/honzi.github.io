Honzi/Guides/EVE Online/Hunting Deployables
Steps to find and kill a deployable:
  1. Find a deployable object to kill via your directional scanner. Any ship can be used.
  2. Scan and bookmark the deployable using a scanning ship.
  3. Return to the bookmark with a DPS ship and destroy it.
