Honzi/Guides/EVE Online/Daily Goals
  • For skillpoints: complete the 2 quickest available dailys.
  • For evermarks and isk: complete as many dailys as possible.
  • To convert evermarks to isk: spend 2,500 evermarks to complete 1 daily that you wouldn't otherwise be able to do. Must be the first daily you complete that day.
  • Recommended fits: Stratios-Dailys for omega and Vexor-Dailys for alpha.
Daily Goal Difficulty Tips for Quick Completion
Armor Repair other Capsuleers Medium Damage and repair an alt or a trusted friend in a ship with at least 2,500 armor hp.
Capture/Defend Contested FW Complex Hard Recommended to skip unless you are actively participating in faction warfare.
Complete 3 Jumps Easy Either do while traveling to another daily in a different system, or set destination 2 jumps away from your current station and back.
Destroy 25 non-capsuleers Medium Do security missions or find npc spawns in asteroid belts in <=0.8 systems.
Gain 50 LP for any corporation Easy Do security missions if you also have to destroy 25 non-capsuleers, otherwise do distribution missions.
Manufacture an Item Easy The cheapest item to create is Nova Rockets from a 10/20 BPO. If you're on an alt with no isk, use the reward from other dailys to pay for it.
Scan 5 Signatures Easy Rookie systems have enough easily scannable signatures.
Shield Boost other Capsuleers Medium Damage and repair an alt or a trusted friend in a ship with a lot of shield hp and slow shield regeneration.
