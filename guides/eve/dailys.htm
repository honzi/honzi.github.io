Honzi/Guides/EVE Online/Daily Goals
  • Always complete at least 2 dailys on every character.
  • If you only want skillpoints, complete the 2 quickest available dailys.
  • If you want evermarks and a little extra isk, complete as many dailys as possible.
Daily Goal Tips for Quick Completion
Armor Repair other Capsuleers Damage and repair an alt or a trusted friend in a ship with at least 2,500 armor hp.
Capture/Defend Contested FW Complex Recommended to skip unless you are already part of FW.
Destroy 25 non-capsuleers
  • Security missions are a great way to complete this daily.
  • You can also find rat spawns in asteroid belts in systems with <= 0.8 security.
Gain 50 LP for any corporation
  • Complete level >= 2 distribution missions.
  • If you also have to destroy 25 non-capsuleers, do security missions.
Manufacture an Item The cheapest item to create is Nova Rockets.
Scan 5 Signatures Rookie systems always have enough easily scannable signatures to easily complete this daily.
Shield Boost other Capsuleers Damage and repair an alt or a trusted friend in a ship with a lot of shield hp and slow shield regeneration.
