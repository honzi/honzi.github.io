Honzi/Guides/EVE Online/Daily Goals
  • Always complete at least 2 dailys on every character.
  • If you only want skillpoints, complete the 2 quickest available dailys.
  • If you want evermarks and a little extra isk, complete as many dailys as possible.
Daily Goal Tips for Quick Completion
Armor Repair other Capsuleers Damage and repair an alt or a trusted friend in a ship with at least 2,500 armor hp.
Capture/Defend Contested FW Complex Recommended to skip unless you are already part of faction warfare.
Destroy 25 non-capsuleers Complete security missions or find rat spawns in asteroid belts in systems with <=0.8 security.
Gain 50 LP for any corporation Do security missions if you also have to destroy 25 non-capsuleers, otherwise complete a distribution mission of level >=2.
Manufacture an Item The cheapest item to create is Nova Rockets from a 10/20 BPO.
Scan 5 Signatures Rookie systems have enough easily scannable signatures to complete this daily.
Shield Boost other Capsuleers Damage and repair an alt or a trusted friend in a ship with a lot of shield hp and slow shield regeneration.
