Honzi/Guides/EVE Online/Distribution Missions
  • If you are running distribution missions that have a chance to go to lowsec/nullsec and you wish to avoid leaving highsec, save your 4 hour decline timer for missions that leave highsec.
  • If you are running distribution missions that remain in highsec, figure out which distribution missions have the most jumps and save your 4 hour decline timer for those missions.
