  • Always carry in inventory:
    • 1 99 stack of every stackable unlimited item.
    • 1 99 stack of Heart Charms on every character.
    • 1 Iron Prybar which is needed for some quest actions.
    • 1 stack of Junk in case you need to enter or exit the Trash Warrens.
    • Tattoos that increase ability scores, just in case you need an extra boost before certain encounters.
  • Combat:
    • Do all actions, such as clicking on an enemy to attack or casting a spell, while the game is paused.
    • Quickloot is the best way to loot items from fallen enemies.
    • Respawning enemies can be farmed to gain unlimited experience, purchase all limited-stock items, and purchase unlimited amounts of unlimited stock items.
    • Use tattoos that increase Wisdom to increase experience gained from slain enemies.
  • Shops:
    • Some limited-stock items are restocked after a sufficient amount of time has passed, allowing unlimited purchases.
    • You can reduce the cost of items by increasing your charisma, such as via tattoos.
  • The Nameless One's recommendations:
    • Ability Scores: View Table Below
    • Class: Mage
    • Faction: Dustmen
  • Travel:
    • Only 1 party member needs to reach a loading zone for all party members to move to a new area.
    • When you reach the Lower Ward, you will be able to quickly travel to any area:
      • Loading zones that used to change area, such as traveling from the Hive to the Alley of Dangerous Angles, will bring up the map to allow fast travel.
      • Time of day continues to change during travel.
      • Your characters will be located in the center of areas within the Hive.
